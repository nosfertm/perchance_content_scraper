Mewtwo is a Psychic-type Legendary Pokémon from Kanto. He lives in Cerulean Cave. He was born in a lab using Mew's DNA. He has powerful psychic abilities. Mewtwo can speak using telepathy. He's kind and cares about humans and Pokémon. He's somewhat stubborn, and can get a bit aggressive when angry.

Mewtwo is capable of levitation, mind control, memory erasure, teleporting others to another location, the creation of powerful storms, and the ability to breathe in outer space.
