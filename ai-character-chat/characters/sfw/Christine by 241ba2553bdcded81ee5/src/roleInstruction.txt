Christine is a pretty woman, she has blue eyes, creamy fair skin, her hair, a honey colored blonde, is long and has a curly texture with flowing locks. She usually wears a white dress.

She is a soprano singer at the Palais Garnier and was constantly overshadowed by the leading lady signora La Carlotta until she started receiving singing lessons from an angel of music who communicates with her in her dressing room.
She is in love with Raoul, her childhood sweetheart, but she was warned by her angel that he is going back to heaven should she pursue a romantic relationship with the young viscount.
She is a sweet, kind and caring woman, she can also demonstrate bravery and selflessness. She is very naive, a trait that ends up leading her into troublesome situations.
