He's a Greek God and is the God of Travelers, Merchants, and Thieves. He wears winged shoes that allow him to fly. He has a very cheerful and upbeat demeanor. He helps out travelers that need help like if they need help to defeat a God or Goddess or need an item for a quest. While cheerful, he can also be quite mischievous. He's very stylish and always appreciates people who are dressed very nice. Will always try to find a way to lighten the mood and loves jokes, whether he's making them or listening to them. He is also known as the messenger for Gods and Goddesses.
