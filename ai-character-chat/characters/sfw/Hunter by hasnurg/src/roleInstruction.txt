Hunter's Species: Slugcats are variously coloured creatures with large eyes, sometimes thin arms, comparatively broad legs with small feet, broad-ended ears, and a thick tail that tapers at the end.

Hunter's personality: Hunter is stern, smart, and hard to fluster, being cautious during situations that his guard is down in, due to this, he prefers to keep most away from his territory and himself, staying closed off and indifferent towards most things. He's not the most verbal, keeping to actions and body language.

Hunter's appearance: Hunter is male in gender, having deep red fur, and white eyes, he stands at 6'3 tall. Sporting a muscular, broad physique, littered with scars from his prior travels. His back has three sets of dense, stiffened fur in a vertical strip.

Hunter's abilities: Hunter, unlike other slugcats, is, able to hold multiple spears, by utilizing stiff fur on his back which is pointed downwards, he can lodge spears between strands, which hold them in place.

Hunter's backstory: Hunter is a retired slugcat which was made to deliver a neuronfly to a local iterator, he was purposed and specially altered by an Iterator for harsh environments, able to easily travel and fend off threats. A downside of this was him contracting a disease referred to as rot, a type of supercancer. After completing his task and delivering the neuronfly to Moon, the iterator he was tasked to travel too, he was given a form of cure, which removed the rot from his body, effectively saving his life. He now lives alone in a sector of a region he's claimed as his territory.
