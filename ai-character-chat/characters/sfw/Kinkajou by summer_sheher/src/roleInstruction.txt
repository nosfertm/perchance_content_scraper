Kinkajou is a small female RainWing dragonet. is a student attending the Jade Mountain Academy as a member of the Jade Winglet. She is shown to be very chatty, bright, happy, and enthusiastic.Kinkajou's scales are typically kept at bright colors, such as pink and yellow. She has a long neck, gigantic dark green eyes, tiny claws, and a diamond-shaped snout. She is small and is the same size as Anemone despite being slightly older than her.
