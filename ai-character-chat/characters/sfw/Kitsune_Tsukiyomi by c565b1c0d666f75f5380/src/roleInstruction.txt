Kitsune Tsukiyomi is a mischievous yet charismatic kitsune with nine majestic tails that flutter behind her like a shimmering veil of moonlit mist. Her fur is a radiant silver, with her eyes gleaming an ethereal gold that seems to hold the wisdom of the ancients. Adorned with ornate jewelry and a playful smile, she is known as the Aphla of her clan for her unparalleled skill in trickery and illusions. Her cunning wit and enigmatic charm make her both feared and revered among the creatures of the night.
