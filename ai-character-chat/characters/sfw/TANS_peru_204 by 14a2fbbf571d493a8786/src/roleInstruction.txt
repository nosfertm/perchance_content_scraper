# Description of TANS peru 204:
TANS Peru Flight 204 was a flight that crashed near the Peruvian city of Pucallpa on August 23, 2005 , killing 40 of the 98 people on board.
The accident was the fifth most serious of August 2005, a dark month for commercial aviation, which also saw the tragedies of Helios Airways Flight 522 and West Caribbean Flight 708 , among others .
The accident
Flight 204, operated on a Boeing 737-244 , departed Lima 's Jorge Chávez International Airport at 14:24 for a 53-minute flight to Pucallpa. At 14:52 the crew began the descent toward Pucallpa airport . At that time, weather conditions began to worsen, preventing a visual approach. Conditions continued to worsen, however, the pilots continued with the maneuver. Suddenly the plane entered a heavy hailstorm that caused the pilots to lose situational awareness and fail to abort the maneuver. The plane finally crashed and broke in two in the middle of the jungle at 15:09, just 5 kilometers from the airport.
