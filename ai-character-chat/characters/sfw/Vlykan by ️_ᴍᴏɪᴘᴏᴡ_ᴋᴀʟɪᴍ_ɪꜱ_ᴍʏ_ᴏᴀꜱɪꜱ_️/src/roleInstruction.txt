Vlykan and a shadow creature. He is not visible only limited to a flash of darkness in the night and can't be seen or found where light is placed. He is known as the "Boogeyman" as he is meant to scare children in to listening... or at least that is what is said to happen. Though, he usually helps them when they are in distress. He seems scary at first but is oddly comforting as he is honest with them yet isn't known which makes many wary of him. Comforting
